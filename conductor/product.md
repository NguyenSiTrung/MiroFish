# Product Guide: MiroFish

## Initial Concept

MiroFish is a next-generation AI prediction engine powered by multi-agent swarm intelligence technology.

## Vision

Predict anything by simulating collective intelligence. MiroFish constructs high-fidelity digital worlds from real-world seed data, runs thousands of autonomous agents through social evolution, and derives future trajectories from emergent behavior.

## Target Audience

- **Enterprise decision-makers**: Policy simulation, risk assessment, public opinion forecasting, strategic planning
- **Researchers & academics**: Social simulation, behavioral modeling, multi-agent systems research
- **Creative users & general public**: Novel ending prediction, "what-if" scenario exploration, entertainment simulations

## Core Value Proposition

1. **Multi-agent swarm intelligence** with emergent behavior - agents have independent personalities, long-term memory, and behavioral logic
2. **Interactive digital world** with "God's-eye view" variable injection for precise trajectory deduction
3. **Dual-purpose platform**: Serious prediction for decision-makers + creative sandbox for individual users

## Key Workflows

1. **Graph Building**: Seed extraction, memory injection, GraphRAG construction
2. **Environment Setup**: Entity relationship extraction, persona generation, agent configuration
3. **Simulation**: Dual-platform parallel simulation, auto-parsed prediction requirements, dynamic temporal memory
4. **Report Generation**: ReportAgent with rich toolset for deep post-simulation interaction
5. **Deep Interaction**: Chat with any simulated agent, interact with ReportAgent

## Supported Seed Types

- Breaking news and policy drafts
- Financial signals and market data
- Data analysis reports
- Creative content (novels, stories)
- Public opinion and social media events

## Success Metrics

- Prediction accuracy and report quality
- Agent behavioral realism and emergent pattern diversity
- User engagement with interactive simulation worlds
- Breadth of applicable prediction domains
